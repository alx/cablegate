SUBJECT: INFLUENTIAL WOMEN IN ICELAND 
 
REF: STATE 202341 
 
¶1.  The following is Embassy Reykjavik's response to reftel 
requesting a list of five of the most influential women in Iceland. 
Embassy Reykjavik's point of contact for women's issues is Political 
Officer Brad Evans (evansbr@state.gov). 
 
¶2.  Embassy has chosen the following five women, weighing factors 
including diversity of profession and background, name recognition, 
and breadth of appeal.
