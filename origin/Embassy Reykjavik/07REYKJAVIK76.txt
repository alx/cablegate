¶1. (SBU) On March 19 DCM and the Acting Political Officer presented 
points contained reftel plus text of Global Initiative Statement of 
Principles to MFA Arms Control Representative Petur Thorsteinsson. 
Russian DCM was informed of our planned demarche but did not 
participate since he had approached the MFA on this issue without 
first consulting us. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Thorsteinsson was very interested in the Global Initiative 
and supports Iceland becoming a partner.  He will inform us when a 
GOI decision is made.
