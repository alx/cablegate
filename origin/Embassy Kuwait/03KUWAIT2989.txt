SUBJECT: DELIVERY OF BAGHDAD COMMERCIAL AIR SERVICE NOTICE 
 
REF: STATE 193275 
 
On July 4, Econ Couns conveyed to Kuwait Airways Corporation 
(KAC) Chairman, General Manager, and  Director of Aviation 
Services reftel's information concerning the Office of the 
Coalition Provisional Authority (OCPA) notice soliciting air 
carriers to apply to provide scheduled passenger-only 
commercial air service to Iraq.  KAC staff also were given a 
print out of the Department-provided notice as proposed for 
posting on the OCPA website (www.cpa-iraq.org). Staff of KAC 
Chairman Ahmad Faisal Al-Zabin informed Embassy on July 7 
that senior KAC leadership would decide that day on whether 
to respond to OCPA's solicitation.
