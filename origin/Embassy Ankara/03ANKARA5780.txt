The Embassy received a fax message from the Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs 11 September requesting assistance to 
transport two busses of Turks traveling to Iraq to attend the 
Third Turkoman General Assembly, which is being held in 
Kirkuk, 13-16 September.  They are requesting appropriate 
security measures be provided for the group that will travel 
through Iraq and enabling their swift and safe transit to 
Kirkuk.  Below is a list of names of the bus drivers, license 
numbers of the buses, and passengers.
