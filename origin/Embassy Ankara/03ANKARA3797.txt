Econoff presented reftel points and the U.S. proposal on 
trade facilitation to the Foreign Trade 
Undersecretariat, MFA, and the Customs Undersecretariat. 
Semine Uygun, Head of the WTO Department at the Foreign 
Trade Undersecretariat, told us on June 10 that 
interagency consultations on this issue are ongoing, and 
that the GOT was not likely to formulate a position on 
this proposal until July.
