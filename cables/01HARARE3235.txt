SUBJECT: MEDIA REPORT COMMONWEALTH MINISTER'S VISIT TO 
ZIMBABWE; HARARE 
 
 
¶1.   THE INDEPENDENT WEEKLY "ZIMBABWE INDEPENDENT" 
   DEDICATED ITS OCTOBER 25 EDITORIAL TO THE VISIT TO ZIMBABWE 
   THIS WEEK BY A TEAM FROM THE COMMONWEALTH.  UNDER HEADLINE 
   "OPEN LETTER TO COMMONWEALTH MINISTERS," THE PAPER 
   COMMENTS: 
 
¶2.   "DURING YOUR FLEETING VISIT TO ZIMBABWE THERE WILL 
   HAVE BEEN A CONCERTED ATTEMPT IN OFFICIAL CIRCLES TO PULL 
   THE WOOL OVER YOUR EYES.  YOU WILL HAVE BEEN TOLD THAT THE 
   ZIMBABWE GOVERNMENT IS PROCEEDING TO ACQUIRE LAND IN A 
   LEGAL WAY, 'AS IT ALWAYS HAS DONE.'  YOU WILL BE TOLD THAT 
   THERE IS FREEDOM OF THE PRESS AND THAT ELECTIONS ARE 
   CONDUCTED ALONG LINES THAT PRODUCE A FREE AND FAIR POLL. 
   IF YOU BELIEVE THAT YOU WILL BELIEVE 
   ANYTHING. . .  WE HOPE YOU WILL PROVE TO BE FRIENDS OF 
   ZIMBABWE. . .AND SPELL OUT WHAT NEEDS TO BE DONE." 
 
SULLIVAN
