(SBU) Icelandic MFA Counselor for Human Rights Ingibjorg 
Davidsdottir informed Poloff on 28 August that the GOI would support 
Ruth Wedgwood for re-election to the UN Human Rights Committee at 
the September 7 election.  Davidsdottir (previously assigned to 
Iceland's mission in Geneva) noted her positive impression of 
Professor Wedgwood's service on the committee and said the GOI was 
happy to support her as a candidate.
