 ¶1.  (U) IN RESPONSE TO REF B, EMBASSY REPORTED THAT THE GOI 
WAS NOT LIKELY TO CONTRIBUTE ITS MADRID-PLEDGED IRAQ 
RECONSTRUCTION FUNDS TO THE INTERNATIONAL FINANCE 
CORPORATION'S SMALL BUSINESS FINANCE FACILITY FOR IRAQ (REF 
    E).  WE HAVE ALSO DESCRIBED ITALY'S OVERALL RECONSTRUCTION 
PRIORITIES AND THE GOI'S PREFERENCE FOR USING ITS FUNDS TO 
SUPPORT PRIMARILY BILATERAL PROJECTS (REF C). 
 
¶2. (U) AS INSTRUCTED IN REF A, EMBASSY WILL AGAIN MAKE THE 
CASE FOR A GOI CONTRIBUTION TO THE FACILITY.  OUR PRIMARY 
INTERLOCUTOR ON THE INTERAGENCY IRAQ TASK FORCE IS NOW 
TRAVELING IN THE UNITED STATES WITH ITALIAN U/S MANTICA (REF 
D).  WE WILL REPORT THE GOI RESPONSE TO OUR SECOND DEMARCHE 
AS SOON AS WE HAVE IT.
