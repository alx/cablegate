SUBJECT:  Turkish MFA Proposes Mutual Support for 
Candidacies in the IMO 
 
 
REF:  State 281005 
 
 
¶1. This message contains an action request in para 3. 
 
 
¶2. Econoff delivered reftel demarche seeking Turkish 
support for the U.S. candidacy to the International 
Maritime Organization (IMO) Council in Category "A" to 
Orkun Ersoy, of MFA's Maritime and Aviation Department, 
on October 7.  Ersoy responded to the demarche by 
proposing mutual support:  Turkish support for the U.S. 
candidacy in exchange for U.S. support of Turkey's 
candidacy in Category "C". 
 
 
¶3. Action Request:  Please advise Embassy as to how to 
respond to MFA's proposal.  Edelman
