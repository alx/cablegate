SUBJECT:  ICELAND:  WEDGWOOD UNDER SERIOUS CONSIDERATION 
 
REF:  STATE 85722 
 
¶1.  Post presented reftel diplomatic note, CV, and talking points to 
Icelandic MFA Human Rights Officer Ingibjorg Davidsdottir June 13. 
Davidsdottir responded June 19 that the GOI would probably make its 
decision on which candidates to support for the UN Human Rights 
Committee in August.  She added that the MFA is giving Ruth Wedgwood 
"careful and favorable consideration" and would "likely" support 
her. 
 
¶2.  The Ambassador will reiterate the importance that the U.S. 
attaches to the UNHRC and to Professor Wedgwood's service there when 
she shortly pays a courtesy call on new Icelandic Foreign Minister 
Valgerdur Sverrisdottir.
