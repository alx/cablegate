SUBJECT:  GETTING MONEY TO THE TISA 
 
REF: STATE 92142 
 
¶1.  ECONCHIEF CONTACTED UAE CENTRAL BANK OFFICIALS ON 
THE MORNING OF APRIL 12, THE FIRST WORK DAY AFTER THE 
RECEIPT OF REFTEL.  SULTAN AL-SAKEB, THE HEAD OF THE 
CORRESPONDENT BANKING DIVISION (WHICH IS RESPONSIBLE FOR 
MATTERS PERTAINING TO FACILITATION OF TRANSFERS TO THE 
TISA IN AFGHANISTAN), STATED THAT THE MOST EXPEDITIOUS 
MEANS TO EFFECT THE PROPOSED TRANSFER WOULD BE FOR THE 
FUNDS TO BE DEPOSITED - AS HAS BEEN THE STANDARD 
OPERATING PROCEDURE FOR SOME TIME - INTO THE UAE CENTRAL 
BANK'S ACCOUNT MAINTAINED AT THE NEW YORK FED.  THE 
ACCOUNT NUMBER THERE IS 021083077, REPEAT, 021083077. 
THE USG FOUR MILLION USD TRANSFER TO THE TISA OUTLINED 
REFTEL SHOULD BE ANNOTATED "FOR TRANSFER TO MINISTRY OF 
FINANCE AFGHANISTAN, ACCOUNT NUMBER 1000-103700-002." 
 
¶2.  AL-SAKEB CONFIRMED THAT ONCE THE MONIES ARE 
DEPOSITED INTO THE UAE CENTRAL BANK'S ACCOUNT IN NEW 
YORK, HE WOULD BE ABLE TO EFFECT THE TRANSFER TO THE 
TISA. 
 
¶3. MINIMIZE CONSIDERED.
