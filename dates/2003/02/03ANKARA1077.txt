SUBJECT: TURKISH RESPONSE ON WTO NON-MARKET ACCESS PROCESS 
 
 
REF: STATE 19378 
 
 
Econoff delivered reftel update on the WTO non-market access 
process to Oya Karakas, MFA Head of Department for 
Multilateral Economic Affairs and Semine Uygun, Head of 
Department for WTO Issues at the Foreign Trade 
Undersecretariat on February 6.  Both Karakas and Uygun 
responded that the GOT was still considering the U.S. 
proposal to eliminate all non-agricultural tariffs, and had 
not decided whether to support it.
