¶2.  (SBU) Per Reftel, Mission has conveyed the points 
requesting support against one-sided anti-Israel 
resolutions at the UN to the Sri Lankan and Maldivian 
governments.  In light of the importance of this matter, 
we suggest that the U.S. points be communicated directly 
to Prime Minister Wickremesinghe's delegation in 
Washington and to Sri Lanka's UN delegation.
