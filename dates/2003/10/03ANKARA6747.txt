SUBJECT: DEMARCHE OPPOSING CUBA'S UN RESOLUTION AGAINST THE 
EMBARGO:  TURKEY 
 
 
REF: STATE 301451 
 
 
(U) Embassy Ankara Deputy Polcouns delivered reftel demarche 
to Americas Department Head Cihat Enginay on October 28. 
Enginay noted Turkey's vote in favor of the Cuban resolution 
in 2002 but undertook to see whether Turkey would consider 
the reftel talking points, including the U.S. request to 
abstain, in deciding on this year's vote. 
EDELMAN
