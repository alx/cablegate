Classified By: RSO MARCO A. FERNANDEZ 1.4 (C) AND (D) 
 
¶1. (S/NF) On December 15, 2009, American Embassy Reykjavik convened 
an Emergency Action Committee (EAC) meeting as per REFTEL A.  CDA 
Watson chaired the meeting.  In attendance were A/DCM, POL, PAO, 
CONS, ECON, IPC, MILREP, MGT and RSO.  Ref A was discussed by the 
EAC. 
 
¶2. (S/NF) RSO began the meeting by sharing REFTEL A with all the 
members of the EAC.  RSO and CONS discussed REFTEL A and agreed to 
its contents regarding the immediate notification of threat 
information to individuals or corporate entities via the EAC.  The 
EAC was also in complete agreement with the contents of REFTEL A.  No 
further subjects were discussed during the EAC and the EAC was 
convened. 
 
¶3. (SBU) POC is Marco A. Fernandez, RSO, IVG 682 X2289.
