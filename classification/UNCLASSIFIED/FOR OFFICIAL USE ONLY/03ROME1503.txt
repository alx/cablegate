SUBJECT: TFIZ01: UN FLASH APPEAL: ITALY UPDATE ON 
HUMANITARIAN RESPONSE 
 
REF: A. SECSTATE 89192 
 
     ¶B. ROME 1431 
 
¶1.  (SBU) ECONOFF DELIVERED REF A POINTS ON APRIL 8 TO LUIGI 
MACCOTTA, MFA OFFICE DIRECTOR FOR IRAQ AND DEPUTY COORDINATOR 
OF ITALY'S IRAQ TASK FORCE.  MACCOTTA UPDATED EARLIER FIGURES 
OF ITALY'S PLANNED CONTRIBUTION TO IRAQ HUMANITARIAN 
ASSISTANCE (REF B).  HE SAID THE TASK FORCE HAS RECEIVED PM 
BERLUSCONI'S APPROVAL TO SUBMIT FOR PARLIAMENTARY APPROVAL AN 
EMERGENCY FUNDING BILL CALLING FOR EUROS 85 MILLION IN 
ASSISTANCE.  APPROXIMATELY 20 MILLION WILL GO TO BILATERAL 
PROGRAMS, WITH THE REMAINING EUROS 65 MILLION GOING TO THE UN 
FLASH APPEAL.  (NOTE: THESE ARE THE AGGREGATE IRAQ 
HUMANITARIAN ASSISTANCE FIGURES FOR THE GOI SO FAR, AND 
ENCOMPASS SMALLER AMOUNTS FLOATED BY VARIOUS ITALIAN 
OFFICIALS IN DIFFERENT FORA LAST WEEK.  THE FIGURES DO NOT, 
HOWEVER, INCLUDE ITALIAN FUNDS THAT WILL BE DIRECTED TO THE 
UN THROUGH THE EU.
