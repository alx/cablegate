SUBJECT: UK APPROVES ADDITIONAL GBP 11.5 MILLION IN 
ADDITIONAL BUDGET SUPPORT TO PALESTINIAN AUTHORITY 
 
REF: A. LONDON 2697      B. LONDON 2649      C. SECSTATE 120847  1. (SBU) The UK will provide an additional GBP 11.5 million (approximately USD 19 million) in budget support to the Palestinian Authority, Alice Mann, Deputy Head (London) Palestinian Program at the Department for International Development told ECOUNS on December 4. The money will be disbursed this month to the World Bank's Trust Fund and then on to the PA.
