Â¶1.  (SBU)   Post delivered points in reftel to Petur Thorsteinsson 
in the Directorate for External Trade and Economic Affairs at the 
Icelandic Ministry of Foreign Affairs on January 23.  Thorsteinsson 
thanked Emboff for the information regarding the Islamic Republic of 
Iran Shipping Lines (IRISL) and Hafiz Darya Shipping (HDS).  He said 
that the Government of Iceland intends to fully implement the 
provisions of UNSCR 1803 and will remain vigilant regarding IRISL 
attempts to enter Icelandic ports.  Thorsteinsson further stated 
that he would send a notification letter to Icelandic insurance 
agencies about this matter.  They will be properly warned, he said, 
regarding possible attempts by IRISL or HDS to obtain liability 
insurance coverage.
