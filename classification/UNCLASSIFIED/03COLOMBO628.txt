SUBJECT:  Mission deploys points re in-kind offers of 
humanitarian assistance for Iraq 
 
Ref:  State 91561 
 
(U) Per Reftel, Mission conveyed on April 10 the points 
on in-kind offers of humanitarian assistance for Iraq to 
the Sri Lankan and Maldivian governments.  As of yet, 
neither government has made any in-kind offers as far as 
we know, but we thought it important that they have our 
points on this issue.
