SUBJECT: ANTI-CORRUPTION CONFERENCE IN KOREA: TURKEY 
PARTICIPATION 
 
 
REF: STATE 103851 
 
 
¶1. (U) Sun Kim, Second Secretary at the Korean Embassy in 
Ankara, said his Embassy recently sent its formal invitation 
to the GOT for the 3rd Global Forum on Fighting Corruption, 
to be held in Seoul May 29-31.  He said the Korean Embassy 
did not receive instructions from Seoul to jointly approach 
(with us) host government. 
 
 
¶2. (U) Kim said the GOT will be sending two or three 
officials from the Ministry of Justice, led by Justice 
Minister Cemil Cicek.  While the GOT delegation will attend 
the 3rd Global Forum on Fighting Corruption, Kim did not know 
whether the delegation would also be attending the 11th 
International Anticorruption Forum, which will be held in 
Seoul May 25-28, prior to the 3rd Global Forum.
